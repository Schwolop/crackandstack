Welcome to Crack and Stack!

Crack and Stack is an upcoming card game from Triple Point Publishing. This game lets you solve its puzzle cards and then race to find the solution hidden in the card's border.

There are two types of puzzle cards:

Semordnilaps use this symbol: and require you to spell the clue word backwards to get the answer.

Anagrams use this symbol: and require you to find the ONE valid anagram of the clue word. The anagram will always start with the same letter as the clue.

Once you've typed in the solution (and got it right!) the border will be revealed. Try to find the hidden answer as fast as you can!
